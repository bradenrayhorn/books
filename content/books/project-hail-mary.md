I read Andy Weir's _The Martian_ probably 10 years ago now. I remember reading the entire book in a day or two. This book is similar. Relatively short chapters with a lot going on leave the pages flying by. Further accentuated by the fact I was reading a large print edition.
Always wanting to read another chapter to see what happened next left me staying up too late a few times.

{{< spoiler reason="major story" >}}
The major dilemmas Rocky and Grace faced were gripping. Grace going back to save Rocky at the end was touching. I liked the ending of only seeing what happened on Grace’s planet and the only closure from Earth being that the Sun is back to normal light output.
{{< /spoiler >}}

At times the humor and the science geek talk felt overdone to me.
